Hiking Tips & Resources

Safety First

  • Check the weather: Rio's weather can change quickly. Avoid hiking during heavy rain.
  • Go with a friend: Never hike alone, especially on more remote trails.
  • Stay on the path: This protects the environment and prevents you from getting lost.
  • Start early: Rio gets very hot. Aim to finish your hike before midday if possible.

Essential Gear

  • Hydration: Carry at least 2 liters of water.
  • Footwear: Use proper hiking shoes or sneakers with good grip.
  • Sun protection: Wear a hat and apply sunscreen regularly.
  • Communication: Keep your phone charged and consider a portable power bank.

Respect Nature

Rio's trails are located within Protected Areas. Please follow the "Leave No Trace" principles:

  • Take all your trash with you.
  • Do not feed the animals.
  • Do not pick plants or flowers.
