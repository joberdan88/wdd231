Rio de Janeiro Hiking Trails Guide

Site Plan

Site Name

Rio Trails Guide

Selected because it is descriptive, easy to remember, and focuses on the core offering: a guide to the best hiking spots in Rio de Janeiro.

Site Purpose

The Rio Trails Guide aims to provide a comprehensive resource for locals and tourists who want to explore Rio de Janeiro's natural beauty through hiking. It offers detailed trail descriptions, safety tips, equipment recommendations, and a community-driven directory of routes.

Scenarios

  1. "I'm a beginner hiker looking for a safe trail with a great view for my first time in Rio. Which one should I choose?"
  2. "What specific gear do I need for a long hike like Pedra da Gávea to ensure safety and comfort?"
  3. "Where can I find reliable information about trail difficulty and estimated time for completion?"

Color Scheme

Primary: Forest Green (#2d5a27)
Secondary: Sandy Yellow (#f4a460)
Accent: Earthy Brown (#8b4513)
Background: Light Green (#f0fdf4)

Used on: Headings (Primary), Buttons/Links (Secondary), Borders/Emphasis (Accent), Page Background (Background).

Typography

Montserrat: Used for all headings to provide a modern, bold, and adventurous look.

Roboto: Used for body text and navigation for high readability across all devices.
